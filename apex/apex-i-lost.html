Apex I Lost

Lost Payload!

£50 reward

The balloon was launched at 1200 BST on Saturday 25/04/09 from Bromyard, 12mi west of Worcester.

It is a black box with a camera on the outside and a large orange parachute attached (see pic).

It travelled north towards Shrewsbury where we lost track of it. The next GPS beacons came in from XXXX so it could be as far north as Scunthorpe, as far south as Peterborough, or could be somewhere around Nottingham.

The last known position was around 34,000ft at coordinated 52.82666°, -0.455° - about 12km southeast of Grantham.

It's transmitting on 446.05625 MHz; this is PMR446 walkie-talkie channel 5. If you around the area, please turn on your walkie talkies and listen on this channel! If the payload is near you, you will hear modem type noises, once a minute and lasting about 2 seconds.

It is also transmitting a low-power beacon on 433.920 Mhz every 10 seconds.

If you hear anything, or have seen it, or have any other information at all, please contact us at jon.sowman@gmail.com.

There is a £50 reward if you locate it!
